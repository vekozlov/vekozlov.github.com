Last projects I worked on

Questplanet - this project provides a city adventure services in Russia. Process of an adventure consists of a certain number of tasks, which are placed at the one of the popular city's tourist routes. All interactions are passed through mobile devices. City quests are devided to categories, payed by credit cards or electronic money.

I built the project from scratch. Made backend logic on Ruby on Rails, tested the app via Rspec (> 500 tests) and maintain the project.

The app is available on www.questplanet.ru

Impacthealth is a revolutionary step in healhcare. Webservice, that help people to pay less for their healthcare plans. After answering to couple easy questions, the algorithm searches every plan to find the best, most affordable one to meet user's needs. It helps to save a lot of money for their health enuranse plan.

I worked on front-end here on previous versions of the app.

The app is available on www.joany.com

Tipmetric is a service for restaurants to manage tips distributions. It’s a clever way to manage tips, employees and workload. Restaurants can finally forget old excel sheets workflow that caused a lot of mistakes.

At first I was building the MVP and then client decided to continue project with me and to build fully functional service for restaurant managers and payrolls.

The app is available on www.tipmetric.com
