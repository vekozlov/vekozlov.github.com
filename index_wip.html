Hi. My name is Vyacheslav Kozlov and I'am a full-stack Ruby-on-rails developer.


Approach

What do I want

My main goal is to translate business idea and business requirements into innovative application architecture, that solves real people's problem. It should make a difference, a real deference before and after.

It is not all about programming

If you are a full stack developer, you need more than one programming language. You need to have an eye for design, to know the basics of psychology, management, marketing and business logic. I have all of these. My client gets a full-stack ruby-on-rails developer with marketing background and wide range of adjacent knowledges.

Out of the box.

Technologies stack

I am a full stack developer and can do both backend and frontend. From deploying to server to building all javascript logic in a project.

Front end

I use coffescript for compiling a javascript/jquery and backbone + marionette for fronted and rails for backend.

I am not a designer and do not want to be. However, there are situations, when there no pre-existing mock-ups available. I can easily continue the existing design's guideline and build my own if it is needed. I like clear and simple design layouts that help to bring the idea of the app in more fast and efficient way. The minimum kit every site should have is good pictures, fonts, right color scheme and some icon-pack like font-awesome.

Backend

I am familiar with most popular gems like devise, nokogiri, omniauth, rspec, cucumber etc. Not a big fan of haml/slim when it comes to templates. Html-markup is simple-enough and quite readable.

Test coverage is the best thing that can happen in a life of every application. I use TDD & BDD for almost every app.

In total, I can develop, test and deploy rails applications by myself.
